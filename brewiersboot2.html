Harrisburg Breweries

Brief History.

Brewying in Harrisburg has been around in the city of harrisburg since the earliest of years. Since 1830 there have been many breweries in the area with the highest point was a whooping 2300 in 1880. While many of them have gone away Harrisburg still has thriving breweries and a great spot to hang out with family and friends.
